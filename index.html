The grow10X Platform

An enterprise-grade AI architecture in five integrated layers

Designed with modularity, scalability, and security at its core — working seamlessly together to deliver AI-driven solutions for businesses of all sizes.

01

Interaction Layer

Dynamic, responsive user experiences through modern frameworks like Next.js, React, and Vercel — intuitive interfaces that engage users effectively.

02

Agent Framework Layer

Intelligent autonomous behaviors powered by LangChain, OpenAI functions, and sophisticated orchestration engines for seamless AI interactions.

03

Model & Inferencing Layer

High-performance AI via a mix of LLM APIs, custom fine-tuned models, and optimized inference frameworks for real-time processing.

04

Data & Knowledge Layer

Structured databases, knowledge graphs, and RAG systems (PostgreSQL, Weaviate, Snowflake) for contextually grounded AI responses.

05

Infrastructure Layer

Cloud-native foundations — Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, and GPU-accelerated environments — supporting demanding workloads with flexibility and reliability.

Integrated Responsible AI principles, Identity & Access Management (IAM), and comprehensive compliance tooling across all layers — ensuring enterprise-grade data governance and ethical AI deployment.
